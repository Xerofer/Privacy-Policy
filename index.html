Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

📋

Overview

Money Wise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the Money Wise mobile application.

Short version: We collect only what's necessary to run the app. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
🗂️

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Money Wise:

  • Account information — your email address and username when you register
  • Financial data — expense records, categories, amounts, and dates that you manually enter
  • Receipt images — photos you choose to upload for AI-powered expense extraction
  • Preferences — your selected currency, language, notification settings, and app theme
  • Profile picture — if you choose to upload one (optional)
⚙️

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Money Wise service:

  • Storing and displaying your expense records across devices
  • Generating spending statistics, insights, and reports
  • Processing receipt images through AI to extract expense items
  • Sending reminders and notifications you have enabled
  • Authenticating your account securely
AI Receipt Analysis: Receipt images are sent to a secure processing server and immediately discarded after analysis. They are never stored permanently or used for training AI models.
🔐

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider with industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Passwords are never stored — authentication uses secure token-based sessions
  • We do not store payment card numbers or sensitive financial credentials
🔗

Third-Party Services

Money Wise uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google Gemini AI — receipt image analysis only (ai.google.dev)
  • Google Play Services — app distribution on Android

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

✅

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — view all data stored about you within the app
  • Correction — edit or update any of your information at any time
  • Deletion — delete individual expenses, or your entire account and all associated data
  • Export — export your expense data in CSV format from within the app
  • Portability — your data belongs to you and can be exported at any time
🗓️

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — including expenses, profile information, and preferences — is permanently deleted within 30 days. Receipt images processed by AI are never retained beyond the duration of a single analysis request.

👶

Children's Privacy

Money Wise is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
